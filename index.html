These are the three main types of gloves:

Semi-Contact

Semi contact gloves are made of durable fine quality artificial leather with pre formed mold for safe punching. Comes with elastic Velcro closure. Ideal for training, sparring and competitions.

Boxing

Boxing gloves are cushioned gloves that fighters wear on their hands during boxing matches. The term also refers to gloves used in training, though these often differ from competition gloves.

MMA

MMA gloves or grappling gloves are small, open-fingered gloves used in mixed martial arts bouts.
